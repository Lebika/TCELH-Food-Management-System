Welcome to the Hostel Food Management System for Administrators

The Hostel Food Management System is a comprehensive tool designed for hostel administrators to manage food counts and oversee student leave data across Thirunagar Hostel, Main Hostel, and New Hostel.

View the real-time count of students present in each hostel, broken down by time periods (morning, afternoon, evening, night).Fetch and review counts for any month and year, with the ability to download reports in PDF format.

Use the data to determine food preparation needs, ensuring efficient and waste-free operations.By offering detailed and dynamic food management tools, our system helps streamline the operations of the hostel and mess committee, ultimately contributing to better resource management and reduced food wastage.
